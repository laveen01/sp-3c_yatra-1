DWARKA YATRA

14th-20th April, 2025

Srila Prabhupada Congregation Coordination Committee (SP-3C)

SP-3C started under the leadership of HG Sundar Gopal Prabhuji, with a vision to make Happy Devotees, More Devotees & Contributing Devotees.

Congregational devotees of various parts of Northern & Western India are participants in this forum. The areas include, but are not limited to, Ghaziabad, Badaun, Bareilly, Rudrapur, Pilukhwa, Delhi (SNS & NS), Gaya, Moradabad, Ludhiana, Kathwada, Sahibabad, Shahjahanpur, and Yamuna Nagar.
